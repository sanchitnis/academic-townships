# Charter of Operations

## Decision model
- Polycentric deliberation with transparent audit trail.
- Committee holds final signoff rights in Phase 1.
- External expert inputs are mandatory for major tensions.

## Voting and arbitration
- Prefer consensus when possible.
- Use weighted voting when consensus fails.
- Preserve unresolved but legitimate tensions in dissensus ledger.

## Release gates
- Gate-based approvals before charter promotions.
